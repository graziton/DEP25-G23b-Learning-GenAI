What is Synthesia?

Synthesia is an AI-powered video generation platform that allows users to create professional videos from text scripts. It uses AI avatars and voiceovers to produce high-quality videos without requiring cameras or actors. Synthesia is widely used for corporate training, marketing, and educational content creation.

With its intuitive interface and support for multiple languages, Synthesia makes video production accessible to everyone.

How Does Synthesia Work?

Synthesia uses advanced AI models to generate lifelike avatars and voiceovers based on your input text. By analyzing the script you provide, it creates a video with synchronized visuals and audio that match your desired tone and style.

Getting Started with Synthesia

  1. Visit the official Synthesia website at synthesia.io.
  2. Create an account or log in.
  3. Select a video template or start from scratch.
  4. Enter your text script (e.g., "Welcome to our company’s onboarding program").
  5. Choose an AI avatar and voice style that matches your needs.
  6. Customize settings such as background, branding elements, and language.
  7. Click "Generate Video" to create your professional video!

You can download the generated video or share it directly from the platform.

Integrating Synthesia via API

# Example in Python
import requests

# Hypothetical API endpoint for Synthesia
url = "https://api.synthesia.io/v1/generate"
headers = {
  "Authorization": "Bearer YOUR_API_KEY",
  "Content-Type": "application/json"
}

data = {
  "script": "Welcome to our onboarding program.",
  "avatar": "Professional",
  "language": "English",
  "branding": {
    "logo": "https://example.com/logo.png"
  }
}

response = requests.post(url, json=data, headers=headers)
print(response.json())

Best Practices & Limitations

  • Be concise: Keep scripts short and clear for better engagement.
  • Acknowledge limitations: While Synthesia excels at generating professional videos, it may require manual editing for highly specific visuals or branding.
  • Tweak settings: Experiment with different avatars and styles to match your audience or brand identity.

FAQs About Synthesia

  • Is Synthesia free? No, Synthesia offers subscription plans based on usage and features.
  • Can I use it for commercial projects? Yes, Synthesia is widely used for creating corporate training videos, advertisements, and more.
  • Does it support multiple languages? Yes, Synthesia supports over 120 languages and accents.
